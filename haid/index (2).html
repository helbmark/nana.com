ABOUT US

Established in 1995, The ABC International School is a dynamic, multi-national, non-denominational day school catering to both native and non-native English-speaking students aged 2-18. Following the National Curriculum of England, our programme is delivered entirely in English, with careful adaptations to ensure appropriate recognition of the school’s Vietnamese operational context. We offer globally recognised examinations, including IGCSEs and International AS/A Levels from Cambridge examination board. Our diverse student body represents 23 nationalities, enriching our cultural tapestry. Our dedicated faculty, recruited from countries such as the United Kingdom, Australia, Ireland, New Zealand, Canada, China, and France, comprises over 75 overseas teachers as of August 2025. With a student enrolment exceeding 650, we pride ourselves on our commitment to academic excellence and holistic development.

OUR STAFF

Members of the teaching faculty and administrative support teams have all undergone a highly competitive, rigorous selection procedure. As a result, the school and
